Guess Who

Instructions

Goal of the Game: Guess your opponent’s mystery person before they guess your mystery person.

To start, choose a name from the dropdown list to be your mystery person. Use the chat box and take turns asking one “yes” or “no” question. Based on your opponent’s answer, use the grid of images to eliminate people who cannot be their mystery person. To eliminate a person, click on their image. It will turn black and white. If you made a mistake, click again and it will return to full color.

Example: you might might ask “Does your person have green eyes?”. If your opponent responds “yes”, you can click on the people’s images with brown or blue eyes to eliminate them. If “no”, then you can eliminate people with green eyes.

If you think you figured out your opponent’s person, you can make a guess instead of asking a question. Tell your opponent you are making your guess, and if you are incorrect, you lose the game. If you are correct, you win!
